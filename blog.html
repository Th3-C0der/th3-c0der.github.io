Hackathon Reflections: Building SkillVox

October 2, 2024

The hackathon has come to an end, and while Ctrl+Alt+Defeat didn't come out on top, the journey was unforgettable. Our project, SkillVox, is a voice-controlled gaming tool aimed at enhancing learning in the skill ecosystem. It allows users to interact with learning modules using voice commands, creating a more dynamic and accessible experience.

Though we didn’t win, we pushed ourselves, learned a lot, and developed an app we’re proud of. This is just the beginning for SkillVox, and we’re excited to take the feedback we received to refine it even further. Sometimes, the lessons learned along the way are more important than the win itself. We’ll be back stronger next time!
